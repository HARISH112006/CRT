Angela's Recipe For The Best Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 21/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add mix until combined into a dough.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hourss
